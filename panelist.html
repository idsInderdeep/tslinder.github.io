INVITATION AS A PANELIST IN THE SPORTS CONFERENCE AND JOB FAIR

AT 10:00AM ONWARD, SEPTEMBER 07 TH , 2019, FICCI, NEW DELHI



This is to inform you that Sports, Physical Education, Fitness & Leisure Skill Council (SPEFL-FC) in partnership with Satan’s Paradise is organizing the Sports Conclave (Sports Rehab- Chapter 05) and Jobs Fair in the Sports Sector on September 07 th , 2019 at FICCI, Federation House, New Delhi. The Inaugural will be held at 10:00 am at Committee Hall/ Hari Prasad Auditorium. The Jobs Fair meetings will be held in the meeting rooms and committee room around post 4 pm onward.

A sport is a powerful activity based in physical athleticism to strengthen social ties and networks. With the aim at focusing on how to encourage more people to play, recreate and participate in sport and what facilities, solutions and infrastructure needed to activate and retain them, we are organizing this sports conference known as “Sports Rehab” along with “Sports Jobs Fair” at FICCI to be held on September 07 th , 2019.

Sports Rehab is one of the platforms connecting all sport lovers and sport fanatics of India in one category, to promote every sport and to stay fit, play, connect, network, recreate, write and share their experience and subsequently numerous sport activities will be organized for all those who have the passion for sports be it in any form. Be it a player, fitness freak, sports blogger, sports equipment vendor, someone who has his own sports business- Sports Rehab welcomes you all.

In this meet, we will have talks with sports investors, venture capitalists, HR experts, sports corporate professionals & policy makers as they will be a part of this seminar being a panelist. It would be our privilege to have you on board as one of the panelist.

The theme of the conference is based on the following topics:


Session 1: Investment opportunities in the upcoming Indian Sports & Fitness Sector
Session 2: Sponsorship Opportunities in Multi Billion Dollars Sports Industry
Session 3: Career Opportunities and Guidance on 1.2 Million Job Opportunities in the Indian Sports Sector

With your wisdom and great experience, we would like to invite you as one of the esteemed panelist for the conference which would boost the morale for many youth & enthusiasts. I look forward for your early confirmation for the above mentioned subject. The programme is enclosed, herewith for your reference.

Yours Sincerely,
